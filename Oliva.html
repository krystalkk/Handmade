The bold and confident Chihuahua is often described as being terrier-like.
His alert nature and suspicion of strangers make him an excellent watchdog. He's sensitive and thrives on affection and companionship.
Chihuahuas often bond to a single person, although they're usually willing to make friends with new people if properly introduced.
Expect them to be a little reserved at first, though. Chihuahuas can be timid if they're not properly socialized as puppies.
Like every dog, Chihuahuas need early socialization — exposure to many different people, sights, sounds, and experiences
— when they're young. Socialization helps ensure that your Chihuahua puppy grows up to be a well-rounded dog.
